Location & Accessibility

How to Get There

Savia Parkway is nestled in Tanza, Cavite — one of the South's fastest-growing municipalities, offering the perfect balance of serene community living and convenient access to Metro Manila.

🚗
By Private Car

Approximately 45 min–1 hour from Metro Manila via CAVITEX or the Manila–Cavite Expressway. Aguinaldo Highway also provides a direct, well-connected route.

🚌
By Public Transport

Buses and UV Express vans from Pasay, Bacoor, or Imus serve Cavite. Jeepneys and tricycles provide last-mile service to Tanza and surrounding barangays.
